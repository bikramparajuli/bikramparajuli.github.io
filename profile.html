Profile

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Geography and Environmental Sustainability at the University of Oklahoma. My work integrates spatial analysis, optimization, GeoAI, and machine learning to create advanced frameworks for facility location modelling and to address complex environmental, weather, and climate challenges.

  • PhD in Geography and Environmental Sustainability, University of Oklahoma (2022–Present) Dissertation: Spatiotemporal Analysis of Historical Drought in the Ganga–Brahmaputra Basin.
  • Master of Engineering in Cartography and Geographical Information Engineering, Wuhan University, China (2019–2021) Coursework in advanced GIS, spatial database management, spatial statistics, photogrammetry, signal processing, and spatial data structures.
  • BE in Geomatics Engineering, Tribhuvan University, Nepal (2014–2018) Coursework in GIS, photogrammetry, remote sensing, environmental modelling, surveying, and engineering mathematics.
