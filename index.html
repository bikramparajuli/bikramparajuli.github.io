Profile

I am an innovative and highly motivated geospatial engineer with academic and research interests in spatial optimization, climate studies, remote sensing, GeoAI, machine learning, and spatial statistics. My work integrates GIScience with atmospheric and environmental applications, particularly mobile radar coverage optimization, drought analysis, and large-scale environmental modeling.
