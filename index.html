Profile

I am a geospatial engineer and PhD candidate specializing in spatial optimization, location analysis, climatology, and remote sensing. My research integrates GIScience, GeoAI, and atmospheric sciences with an emphasis on mobile radar deployment optimization and environmental modelling.

My academic background includes an M.Eng. in Cartography and Geographical Information Engineering from Wuhan University, China, and a B.E. in Geomatics Engineering from Tribhuvan University, Nepal. I have worked extensively with GIS, machine learning, spatial statistics, and environmental modelling.
